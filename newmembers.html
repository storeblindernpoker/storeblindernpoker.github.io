Welcome to
Store Blindern poker

We are a newly founded student association based at the University of Oslo. Store Blindern Poker is a welcoming space for players of all levels to learn, play, and enjoy poker together. All students are welcome, no prior knowledge is necessary to join! We do our best to help new members get familiar with the game and improve over time.

Our main activity is a weekly 2-hour Texas Hold'em session held every Friday. The sessions are part of a semester-long tournament, resulting in a final table where players battle it out for the title of Osloâ€™s best student poker player. Our tournament setup makes it possible for new players to join anytime throughout the semester. Missing one or several sessions is never an issue, but regular attendance is rewarded.

Looking ahead we hope to build upon this by arranging more social gatherings, one-shot tournaments, and other events to bring members together outside of the weekly sessions.

We are also on facebook and discord.
