Welcome to Store Blinder poker

We are a young poker association based at UiO Blindern, created as a place where both new and experienced players can meet, learn, and enjoy the game of poker together. You don't need any prior knowledge to join, everyone is welcome, and we do our best to help new members get familiar with the game and improve over time.

Our main activity is a weekly Texas Hold'em tournament, held every Friday. We use a tournament-style system where all players start with the same amount of chips, no matter when in the semester they join. Each game usually lasts a couple of hours and provides a great mix of competition and fun.

Looking ahead, we hope to build on this by arranging more social gatherings, one-shot tournaments, and other events that bring members together outside of the weekly games.
